“Everyone has in him something divine, something his own, a chance of perfection and strength in however small a sphere which God offers him to take or refuse. The task is to find it, develop it and use it. The chief aim of education should be to help the growing soul to draw out that in itself which is best and make it perfect for a noble use.“
- Sri Aurobindo

At SPS International, It is with these inspiring words of Reverend Sri Aurobindo that we are directing all our efforts towards providing good quality education which may help students enjoy the learning process. Our effort is to groom our students into characters who are mentally strong, morally upright, socially responsible and sensitive to the environment & nature. I believe that it is our sincere responsibility to ensure that our students are competent and capable of pursuing the careers of their choice and competing at the highest level. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and sophisticated facilities, SPS International is committed to contribute to the enchanting task of nation-building. For this very purpose, I look forward to constructive interaction with my students and parents.

With Regards
RAHUL SIROHI
Principal
