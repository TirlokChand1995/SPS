The SPS International faculty broadens the learning experience. The highly-qualified and trained staff not only implements the curriculum but also breeds enthusiasm for learning and questioning. Our multitalented faculty further gives impetus to the learning milieu as the School is a remarkable place to call, ‘Home’ for the better part of the day.
